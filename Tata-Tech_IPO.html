Tata Technologies IPO - Everything You Must Know

Engineering the Future: Tata Tech Enters the Public Market

20 December 2023 • 7 min read

Tata Technologies is driving innovation in automotive and aerospace engineering.

After years of anticipation, Tata Technologies—one of India’s leading engineering and product development firms—is entering the capital market. As a subsidiary of Tata Motors, the company offers digital engineering, product lifecycle management (PLM), and smart manufacturing solutions. Here’s your complete guide to the Tata Technologies IPO and whether it belongs in your portfolio.

About Tata Technologies

  • Founded in 1989, Tata Technologies provides engineering services to global automotive, aerospace, and industrial clients.
  • Clients include Jaguar Land Rover, Airbus, Ford, and major EV manufacturers.
  • It has delivery centers in India, Europe, the USA, and China.
  • Focus areas include electric vehicle design, autonomous mobility, and Industry 4.0 technologies.

Key Strengths

  • Strong backing of Tata Group and strategic synergy with Tata Motors.
  • Global clientele and domain expertise in next-gen automotive engineering.
  • Profitable for several consecutive years with healthy margins.
  • Growing demand for digital engineering and smart mobility solutions.

Risks and Concerns

  • Revenue concentration with top clients like JLR and Ford.
  • Exposed to global economic slowdowns, especially in the automotive sector.
  • Intense competition from global engineering service providers like TCS, LTTS, and Infosys.
