Best Pet Insurance Policies in India

Securing Health & Happiness for Your Furry Companions

03 November 2024 • 6 min read

Pet insurance helps protect your finances and your pet’s well-being.

With growing awareness about pet health in India, more pet parents are now opting for pet insurance. Just like humans, pets may face health emergencies, accidents, or chronic diseases. Having a pet insurance policy ensures financial security and quality care during such events. Here's everything you should know about the best pet insurance options available in India today.

Why Consider Pet Insurance?

  • Veterinary costs are rising sharply—routine visits, surgeries, and diagnostics can cost thousands.
  • Pet insurance covers treatments, surgeries, accidental injuries, and more.
  • Some policies also cover third-party liabilities, loss/theft of pet, and OPD consultations.
  • Peace of mind: You focus on your pet’s recovery, not the expense.

What’s Usually Covered?

  • Accidental injuries and fractures
  • Major illnesses like cancer, skin conditions, liver issues
  • Surgery & hospitalisation expenses
  • Death due to accident or disease
  • Optional: OPD visits, vaccinations, theft or loss cover

What’s Not Covered?

  • Pre-existing illnesses at the time of policy purchase
  • Pregnancy-related expenses or cosmetic surgeries
  • Injuries due to negligence or abuse
  • Certain breeds or pets outside the specified age limit
