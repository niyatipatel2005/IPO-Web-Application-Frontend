IREDA IPO - Everything You Must Know

India’s Renewable Energy Financier is Going Public

20 December 2023 • 4 min read

IREDA is fueling India’s green future—now it’s time for investors to participate.

The Indian Renewable Energy Development Agency (IREDA), a key financial institution in India’s clean energy revolution, launched its IPO to much anticipation. With the government’s push towards a carbon-neutral economy, IREDA’s listing comes at a time when green energy investments are booming. Here's everything you should know before investing.

About IREDA

  • IREDA is a Mini-Ratna (Category I) PSU under the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE).
  • It provides financial assistance to renewable energy and energy efficiency projects across India.
  • Since its inception in 1987, it has financed over 3,000 renewable projects.

Company Strengths

  • Backed by Government of India and operates under MNRE.
  • Focus on fast-growing renewable segments: solar, wind, hydro, bioenergy, and EV infrastructure.
  • Low NPAs and strong asset quality in the lending portfolio.
  • Strategic role in supporting India's 500 GW renewable energy goal by 2030.

Risks to Watch

  • Exposure to interest rate fluctuations and credit risks.
  • Policy changes in renewable energy subsidies or tariffs could impact funding demand.
  • High competition from other infrastructure financing institutions and private lenders.
