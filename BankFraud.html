Types of Banking Frauds - How to Prevent Them

Understanding Banking Scams & Staying Safe

08 December 2023 • 5 min read

Be alert – Banking scams are rising!

Banking frauds are rising with the growing use of digital banking services. From phishing to card cloning, fraudsters use clever tricks to steal your money. Let’s explore the major types of banking frauds and how to stay protected.

Types of Common Banking Frauds

  • Phishing: Fake emails or websites that steal your login credentials.
  • Card Skimming: Devices that clone your card information from ATMs.
  • Vishing: Fraudsters pretending to be bank officials on phone calls.
  • OTP Fraud: Tricksters gain access to your OTPs and complete unauthorized transactions.
  • SIM Swap Fraud: Your phone number is hijacked to access banking OTPs and alerts.

How to Prevent These Frauds

  • Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords with anyone—even if they claim to be bank officials.
  • Use secure networks while doing online banking. Avoid public Wi-Fi.
  • Keep your mobile banking app and antivirus software updated.
  • Immediately block your card or freeze your account if suspicious activity occurs.
  • Check your account statements regularly and report unauthorized transactions.
