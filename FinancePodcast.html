Popular Finance Podcasts in India

Learn Money, Investing, and Wealth Like a Pro

30 November 2023 • 6 min read

Finance learning is just a podcast away!

Podcasts have emerged as one of the most engaging ways to consume knowledge, especially in the field of personal finance. Whether you’re a college student, salaried professional, or an aspiring investor, these podcasts make financial literacy simple, actionable, and even entertaining.

Top Finance Podcasts to Follow in India

  • Paisa Vaisa by Anupam Gupta: One of India’s most loved personal finance podcasts, covering topics from mutual funds to insurance with expert guests every week.
  • Finshots Daily: Short, crisp episodes explaining finance and economics in just 5-7 minutes—perfect for daily commuting.
  • Millennial Paisa: Tailored for young Indians, this podcast focuses on savings, SIPs, and early-stage investing in a fun conversational style.
  • The Musafir Series by CA Rachana Ranade: A podcast about wealth creation, taxation, and startup finance with real-life case studies.
  • Mint Primer: Run by Mint, it provides weekly breakdowns of economic issues, policies, and personal finance queries.

Why Should You Listen to Finance Podcasts?

  • Easy and time-saving way to improve financial knowledge on-the-go.
  • Podcasts feature experts, CEOs, and certified planners sharing real insights.
  • Learn how to manage your salary, EMIs, investments, and taxes.
  • Boosts financial confidence and long-term planning habits.
  • Many podcasts are free and available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, etc.
